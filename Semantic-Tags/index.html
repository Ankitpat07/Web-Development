Tourist Places in Maharashtra

Ajanta and Ellora Caves

The Ajanta and Ellora caves are UNESCO heritage sites famous for their rock-cut architecture and ancient paintings, located in Aurangabad, Maharashtra.

Mahabaleshwar

A beautiful hill station in the Western Ghats, popular for its scenic viewpoints, strawberries, and cool climate.

Festivals of different States

Ganesh Chaturthi (Maharashtra)

Ganesha are worshipped in homes and public pandals before being immersed in water with processions and chants of "Ganpati Bappa Morya!"

Pongal(Tamil Nadu)

A harvest festival celebrated in mid-January to thank the Sun God and nature for a bountiful harvest.

UNESCO World Heritage Sites in India

Qutub Minar(Delhi)

A 73-meter high tower built in the 12th century, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture.

Sun Temple(Konark)

A 13th-century temple shaped like a chariot, located in Odisha.
